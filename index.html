About Us

As technology becomes more and more important to our daily lives, we are a non-profit organization working to provide internet access to all, so that everyone can share the benefits of the electronics we know and love. We are going to do this by:

  • Donating Refurbishing Electronics to those in Need
  • Teaching Seniors How to Use the Internet
  • Developing an App to Teach Cyber Security to All

Because this problem is often overlooked, there aren’t many good solutions out there right now. Some people are simply unable to afford these devices, while other adults can afford the devices but simply can’t figure out how to use them because they never grew up these devices.

This problem is becoming more and more severe, while the benefits of technology only increase.
